Colosseum in Rome, Italy

Reason why this place is special to me: This place is special to me because it's like entering another universe where culture and history come to life. I’ve always been fascinated by the stories behind places like the Colosseum and the Roman Forum — it’s like the past is still present, echoing through the city.

How to get there: The quickest way to reach Rome is by air through its two main airports: Fiumicino (FCO) for international flights and Ciampino (CIA) for budget airlines serving shorter European routes.

Other places to visit in the city/country.

Venice (city in Italy): Venice, often referred to as the "City of Canals", is one of Italy’s most enchanting and unique destinations. Built on over 100 small islands in a lagoon of the Adriatic Sea, Venice is a place where history, art, and romance blend seamlessly with its captivating waterways and stunning architecture.
