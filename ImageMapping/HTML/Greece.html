Santorini's White-Washed Buildings

Reason why this place is special to me: This place is special to me because its stunning views and unique charm feel like something from a postcard, which is why I find it so wonderful. The iconic white building with blue domes, stunning sunsets, and peaceful atmosphere make it feel magical.

How to get there: You can get there by plane or ferry. The fastest way is to fly from Athens to Santorini, which takes about 45 minutes.

Other places to visit in the city/country.

St. Peter’s Basilica (Vatican City): Designed by some of the finest artists in history, including Michelangelo and Bernini, it is a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture. The basilica's large Michelangelo-designed dome dominates the skyline and if you climb to the top provides breathtaking views of Rome.
