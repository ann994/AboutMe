My Kind Of Film

Prepare to laugh loudly and solve fascinating mysteries with my favorite mystery and comedy television series!

Mystery

Lucifer

Synopsis: Morningstar, the Devil, abandons Hell to run a luxurious nightclub in Los Angeles. He soon becomes a consultant for the LAPD.

Director: Len Wiseman (Pilot), Sherwin Shilati, and various others.

Stars: Tom Ellis, Lauren German, D.B. Woodside, Lesley-Ann Brandt

Comedy

Big Bang Theory

Synopsis: The show follows socially awkward physicists Leonard and Sheldon, whose lives change when aspiring actress Penny moves in across the hall. Along with their equally geeky friends.

Director: Mark Cendrowski

Stars: Johnny Galecki, Jim Parsons, Kaley Cuoco, Simon Helberg, Kunal Nayyar
